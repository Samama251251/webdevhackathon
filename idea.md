**AI-Powered Career Preparation Platform**

**Description:**

An intelligent web application that helps students navigate their job search by automatically discovering relevant opportunities based on their resume and providing deeply personalized, research-backed interview preparation for specific technical roles.

**Core Requirements:**

**1. Intelligent Resume Analysis**
* Accept resume uploads in PDF format
* Extract and structure key information: skills, experience, education, projects
* Build a comprehensive user profile from resume data
* Identify technical expertise areas and proficiency levels
* Store structured resume insights for ongoing use

**2. Automated Job Discovery & Matching**
* Scrape job listings from actual sources across the internet
* Filter opportunities based on extracted resume qualifications
* Rank jobs by relevance to user's specific expertise and experience level
* Present curated job matches with compatibility scores explaining why each job fits
* Display key requirements and alignment with user qualifications

**3. Deep Research-Powered Interview Preparation**
* Accept user input for upcoming interview (company name, role, key technologies)
* Perform multi-step AI research process:
    * Search the internet for current, relevant information about the specified technology/role
    * Identify trending topics, common pitfalls, and best practices
    * Analyze what matters most for that specific technical area
    * Filter out outdated or irrelevant information
* Generate comprehensive interview preparation materials:
    * Technology-specific questions (e.g., Node.js event loop, async patterns, middleware)
    * Behavioral questions relevant to the role

---

* Difficulty-appropriate questions based on user's experience level
* Study guide with key concepts to review

**4. User Dashboard & Analytics**
* Visual overview of job search progress and discovered opportunities
* Skill coverage analysis showing strengths and gaps
* Interview preparation tracking and history
* Clear presentation of AI-generated insights and recommendations
